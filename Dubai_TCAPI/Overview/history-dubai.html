Modern History of Dubai

Dubai, the most populous city in the United Emirates, entered its modern era shortly after the conclusion of the First Gulf War. While many agree the war initially had a negative economical impact on the city it was very quick to recover. The recovery is attributed to several factors but in particular the healthy value of oil following both Iraqi conflicts. This reliable source of income allowed the country's leadership to direct their focus on free trade and tourism. Dubai found a great deal of success moving in this direction and today is easily one of the most recognizable modern cities. With their success the ruler of Dubai H.H.S.
