Dubai Metro System Layout

The Dubai metro system is currently comprised of 2 lines, 4 zones, and 49 stations. The lines are identified on maps and in name by colors. The red line, the first constructed line, has 29 stations starting at the Rashidiya station and ending at the UAE Exchange station. The green line has 20 stations starting at the Etisalat station and ending at the Dubai Creek station. Both lines hug the major cities arteries and form somewhat of a “T” shape. The red line runs parallel to the coast, roughly following the path of the major highway, Sheikh Zayed Road. The green crosses the red line twice and envelopes the Dubai Creek.
