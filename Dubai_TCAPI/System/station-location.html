Station Locations

The Dubai Metro system does a fairly good job covering the most traveled to locations in the cities however due to the climate in the region sometimes even a short trip from the station to your final destination can be problematic. For example, arguably Dubai’s most famous landmark, the Burj Khalifa, does not have it’s own stop and the closest stop still requires at least a 10 minute walk. The RTA is handling these shortcomings by employing a system of feeder buses and air conditioned bus stops.

Walking Distances: Landmark to nearest metro station

  • Burj Al Arab 8 km
  • Burj Khalifa 900 m
  • The Palm Atlantis Hotel 13 km
