Dubai Metro Security

As far as security is concerned the RTA has left nothing to chance. Trains are regularly patrolled by a dedicated police force. Throughout the system there are over 3,000 CCTV cameras to monitor any sort of situation that might arise. In the case that the police are not physically present to handle a public safety concern there are Emergency Call Boxes available at every station and on every train car. Response time is guaranteed by a fully integrated radio system between the Metro and emergency civil services.
